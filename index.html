About me

Hey, I’m ducklul, but most people just call me duck. I’ve been coding for about five years, mostly working on backend development. I really enjoy building systems that don’t fall apart, I mean clean, easy to maintain, and designed in a way that actually makes sense so they don’t cause headaches later on.

I feel most at home working on backend systems and internal tools that keep everything running smoothly behind the scenes. That is where I put most of my energy and passion.

I’m always curious about technology and love learning new things. Whether it is watching random tech videos late at night or digging through forums to find the latest news, I am constantly exploring and trying to stay up to date with cool advancements.

When I am not coding, I like to build custom PCs, play video games, and spend time playing my guitar and ukulele. Music and tech are two things that really keep me inspired.

I also enjoy creating content every now and then. Sometimes I make short form videos for TikTok or Instagram, and other times I stream live on Twitch. It is a fun way to share what I am interested in and connect with others.

I am not perfect, but I always put in my best effort. For me, the best work happens when I keep improving and tweaking things until they feel just right.
